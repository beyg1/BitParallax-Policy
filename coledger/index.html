Welcome to CoLedger

CoLedger is a calm financial instrument tailored for small partner-run businesses. It provides a simple, secure way to track shared expenses, manage business accounts, and settle balances with partners directly from your device.

Core Features

  • Contracts & Assigned Expenses: Easily log operational costs and assign clear responsibilities between partners.
  • Settlement Engine: Instantly calculate fair balances for all parties involved without the guesswork.
  • Double-Handshake System: Ensure operations stay trustworthy and conflict-free by requiring mutual agreement on transactions.
